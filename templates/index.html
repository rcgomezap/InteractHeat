The Steady Heat Equation

$$ k \nabla^2 T + q = 0 $$

What is it?

The steady-state heat equation is a partial differential equation (PDE) that describes the distribution of temperature in a given region over time when the temperature field has reached a state of equilibrium (i.e., the temperature does not change with time).
